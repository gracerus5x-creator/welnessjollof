## Quick summary

Welness Jollof is a content website. We do not require accounts, do not store credit-card info, do not sell data, and do not collect or store any health information you share with us beyond what's needed to respond to your email.

## Who we are

**Welness Jollof** (welnessjollof.com), operated by Augustine Usifo, Lagos, Nigeria.
Privacy contact: **info@welnessjollof.com**

## What we collect

### 1. Anonymous analytics
If we use a privacy-respecting analytics tool, it logs:
- Pages visited (which guides are most read)
- General location (country / region — never street-level)
- Browser type and screen size
- Referring website

This data is **aggregated and anonymous**. It cannot be tied back to your identity.

### 2. Affiliate-link click attribution
When you click an affiliate link to a supplement retailer (e.g. iHerb, Amazon), the destination sets a tracking cookie identifying Welness Jollof as the referrer. **We do not see what you purchase** — only that a referral attribution occurred. The retailer's privacy policy governs anything beyond that.

### 3. Voluntary contact
If you email us at info@welnessjollof.com, we receive your email and any info you choose to include — **including any health information you choose to share**. We keep emails for support purposes only and never share them with third parties.

⚠ **Please do not send sensitive medical information via email.** Email is not a secure channel for protected health information.

## What we do NOT collect

- We do not have accounts or logins.
- We do not store payment information (purchases happen on retailer sites, not here).
- We do not run health surveys, intake forms, or symptom-checker tools.
- We do not store, sell, or share any health information.

## Your rights

Under the EU **GDPR**, the UK **DPA**, the Nigerian **NDPR**, the California **CCPA**, and similar laws:

- **Access:** You may ask what data we hold on you. Reply within 30 days.
- **Deletion:** You may ask us to delete data we hold. Done within 30 days unless legal retention applies.
- **Opt-out of analytics:** Most browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal — we honor it. Browser extensions like uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger also work.
- **Cookie control:** Clear or block cookies in your browser settings.

To exercise any right, email **info@welnessjollof.com** with subject "Privacy request".

## Children

Welness Jollof is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

## Health information and HIPAA

We are not a covered entity under HIPAA and do not provide healthcare services. The content here is informational, not clinical. Please do not send protected health information to us; use a HIPAA-compliant channel (your healthcare provider's portal) for those communications.
