## Primary inbox

**info@welnessjollof.com**

This goes to the editorial team. Reply within **2 business days** (Lagos time, UTC+1).

## What to send where

| What you have | Use |
|---|---|
| **Clinical or factual correction** on an existing article | info@welnessjollof.com — subject: "Correction: [article title]" — please include the source you'd like us to verify against |
| **Suggestion for a topic to cover** | info@welnessjollof.com — subject: "Topic suggestion" |
| **Supplement brand / retailer partnership inquiry** | info@welnessjollof.com — subject: "Partnership" |
| **Press / interview / podcast request** | info@welnessjollof.com — subject: "Press" |
| **Privacy concern or data-deletion request** | info@welnessjollof.com — subject: "Privacy" (see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/)) |

## What we cannot help with

- **Personal medical questions.** We are not your healthcare provider. Please direct medical questions to a licensed clinician.
- **Diagnosing conditions** from symptoms you describe in an email.
- **Suggesting specific dosages** for your individual situation — those depend on factors only your doctor or pharmacist can evaluate (current medications, conditions, lab values).

We'll happily point you to general guidance on a topic, but we don't and can't offer individual clinical advice.

## Office location

Operated remotely from **Lagos, Nigeria** (West Africa, UTC+1).

## Response time expectations

- **Editorial corrections:** within 2 business days, and fixes published within 48 hours of confirmation
- **Privacy / data-deletion requests:** within 30 days (legal max under GDPR/NDPR)
- **Partnership proposals:** within 5 business days

## Emergency disclaimer

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, **call your local emergency number now**. This website cannot help with emergencies. In Nigeria: 112. In the US: 911. In the UK: 999.
