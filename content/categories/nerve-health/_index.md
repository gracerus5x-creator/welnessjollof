If you've been searching for relief from burning feet, tingling hands, or the kind of brain fog that follows years of low B12 — you're in the right place.

These guides translate the actual peer-reviewed evidence on **nerve-supportive nutrition** into plain language: which supplements have controlled trials behind them (ALA, methylcobalamin, benfotiamine), which are mostly marketing, and how to talk to your doctor about adding them alongside — not instead of — first-line therapy.

Every article cites its sources, names the dosing used in clinical trials, and flags interactions before benefits.
