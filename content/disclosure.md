## Plain-English summary

**Some product links on Welness Jollof are affiliate links.** When you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at **no extra cost to you**. This commission supports the research time spent reviewing clinical literature, comparing products, and updating our guides.

This disclosure complies with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission (FTC), the UK Advertising Standards Authority (ASA), the Nigerian Federal Competition and Consumer Protection Commission (FCCPC), and similar bodies internationally.

## Programs we participate in

Welness Jollof is a participant in (or has applied to):

- **iHerb Affiliate Program** — supplements and natural-health products
- **Amazon Associates** — supplements, books, and health-related products
- Direct partnerships with select manufacturers when offered

The list updates as we join new programs.

## How affiliate commission affects our coverage

**It does not.** Specifically:

1. We choose what to review based on what readers ask about and what has meaningful clinical evidence — not what pays the highest commission.
2. When we compare products, we compare on **quality and evidence**: third-party testing (USP, NSF, ConsumerLab), bioavailable form, clinically-relevant dosage, and contamination risk. Commission rate does not factor in.
3. If a higher-commission product is clinically inferior, we recommend the lower-commission product. Period.
4. We are willing to recommend free or non-affiliate options when those are genuinely the best choice (e.g. "your local pharmacist may have a cheaper generic" — we'll say that).

## What we will never do

- Recommend a supplement we wouldn't take ourselves.
- Recommend a supplement without clinical evidence behind it.
- Hide negative information about a product we earn commission on.
- Disguise paid placements as editorial.
- Promote "miracle cure" claims or off-label uses for serious conditions.

## Medical disclaimer (worth repeating here)

Affiliate or not, **nothing on this site is medical advice**. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider before starting, stopping, or changing any supplement — particularly if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, taking prescription medications, or managing a chronic condition.

## Cookie-based tracking

When you click an affiliate link, the destination merchant typically sets a cookie identifying Welness Jollof as the referrer. This cookie usually expires 1–30 days later. We do not see the contents of your purchase — only the referral attribution.

You can clear or block these cookies in your browser settings.
